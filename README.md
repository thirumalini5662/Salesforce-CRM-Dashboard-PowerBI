# Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Dashboard in Power BI

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Dashboard designed using Salesforce CRM concepts and developed in Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, lead management, opportunity tracking, customer engagement, and pipeline revenue through interactive visualizations.

The project simulates a real-world CRM environment where leads are converted into accounts, contacts, and opportunities while tracking sales activities and business performance.

---

## Objective

- Monitor the complete sales pipeline
- Track lead conversion and opportunity stages
- Analyze sales representative performance
- Measure pipeline revenue and closed deals
- Support business decisions through interactive dashboards
